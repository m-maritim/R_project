Ask Phase ##2.1 Problem The marketing team is tasked with creating plans to convert casual riders into annual members following the observation by the financial analyst (Moreno) that annual members are more profitable than casual riders. However, to do this effectively, the marketing analysis team (that I am a member of) needs to have a deeper understanding of the differences between annual members and casual riders.

The expected deliverable for this analysis case study are; - A clear statement of the business task - A description of all data sources used - Documentation of any cleaning or manipulation of data - A summary of your analysis - Supporting visualizations and key findings - Your top three recommendations based on your analysis

The Key stakeholders for this case study are; - Cyclistic finance analyst - Director Marketing - The executive team
